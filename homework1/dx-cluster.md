## Verified Servers

* [dx.k3lr.com](telnet://dx.k3lr.com) running AR-Cluster Version 6
* [dxc.w3lpl.net](telnet://dxc.w3lpl.net)

The DX cluster messages are line-oriented and can be accessed via telnet.
